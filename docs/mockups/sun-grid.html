Sun glyphs — ET (across) × temperature (down)

The sun's core colour and glow are driven by temperature (sunshine yellow when cool → terracotta-ochre when hot), while ET (dryness demand) drives the size, ray count & visibility. So rows differ in colour & halo warmth; columns differ in size. The scale is continuous — these cells are sampled examples, not discrete steps.
